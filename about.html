    Who am I?

    I am a Software Engineering and Sociology double major from UC Irvine who is passionate about making a tangible impact on a development team and its platform. I am especially passionate about teams and groups which value the relationships they create with their consumers, as well as their partners. To work on a product and service that makes a meaningful difference in the lives of its users would be nothing but a privilege. I aim to help drive innovation, learn and grow continuously, and thrive in an inclusive environment.
